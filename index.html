About

Lunar Light Studio was a podcast network founded by Blue Lennox and Hannah Boyens in January of 2017, and was partially owned by Hayley Rose. Their mission statement was to create a space for marginalized communities (specifically women, people of color, and LGBTQIA+ individuals) to feel welcome and listened to. Many of the members of Lunar Light Studio were friends of Blue and Hayley.

In late 2020, allegations of psychological, sexual, and emotional abuse by Blue and Hayley came forward from various members of Lunar Light Studio. An intervention was staged on July 28, 2020 in an attempt to make amends and improve their interpersonal relations. This did not work, and Blue and Hayley were ejected from Lunar Light Studio and the streaming group ClownHouse, of which they were part and many former Lunar Light members still are part.

The members of Lunar Light Studio attempted to retrieve the brand and name of the network from Hayley and Blue, but were unsuccessful. They also lost access to the Lunar Light Studio Patreon, from which Hayley and Blue pocketed all of the money. On August 4th, all of the podcasters alerted the fans to this and disassociated themselves from the Lunar Light Studio name, and some time after, the Lunar Light Studio website and social media went dark forever.

Now, some time after the demise of Lunar Light Studio, I remembered it and the collapse of it, and found Pinecast (their feed provider) still hadn't taken down any of the content. I saw a very important opportunity for archival and preservation and I decided to take it. The Lunar Light Studio Archive was born.

The Lunar Light Studio Archive stands with the victims of Blue and Hayley and wishes them nothing but the best. We also do not condone harassment of Hayley or Blue or any of the victims (or anyone else, for that matter). We're all here to have fun and listen to some gay podcasts, let's be nice while we do it.

I Can Fix That

We here at I Can Fix That are committed to three principles. One, all media, no matter how good or bad, can be improved. Two, all media can be improved by making it more gay. Three, we are the only ones who can fix it. Every other week we critique and re-imagine a different property and fix it so you can finally enjoy something for once.
